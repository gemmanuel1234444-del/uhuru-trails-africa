# Uhuru Trails Africa (UTA)

## Overview

Uhuru Trails Africa is a modern digital tourism platform designed to showcase Tanzania's destinations, cultural experiences, accommodation facilities, restaurants, bars, clubs, and adventure activities.

The platform helps travelers discover, explore, and book experiences across Tanzania, including Kilimanjaro, Arusha, Dar es Salaam, Zanzibar, Serengeti, Ngorongoro, Tarangire, and other major tourist destinations.
